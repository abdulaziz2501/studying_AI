SOFTWARE SERVICE AGREEMENT

Section 1 — Definitions
In this Agreement, the following terms have the meanings set forth below:
"Service Provider" refers to DevSolutions Ltd., a technology company.
"Client" refers to any party that purchases services under this agreement.
"Services" means software development, consulting, and maintenance services.
"Deliverables" refers to all work products produced under this agreement.
"Confidential Information" includes all technical, business, and financial information marked as confidential.

Section 2 — Scope of Services
2.1 The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services:
    (a) Custom software development according to agreed specifications
    (b) Technical consulting and architecture design
    (c) System integration and API development
    (d) Quality assurance and testing services
    (e) Maintenance and support for delivered systems

2.2 All services shall be performed in accordance with industry best practices and applicable standards.

2.3 The Client shall provide all necessary access, data, and cooperation required for service delivery.

Section 3 — Payment Terms
3.1 Payment Structure: Services are billed on a time-and-materials basis at rates specified in Schedule A.
3.2 Invoicing: Invoices shall be issued on the 1st of each month for work completed in the previous month.
3.3 Payment Due Date: All invoices are due within 30 (thirty) days of invoice date.
3.4 Late Payment Penalty: Late payments shall incur a penalty of 1.5% per month on the outstanding amount.
3.5 Advance Payment: A 30% advance payment is required before project commencement.

Section 4 — Project Delivery
4.1 Delivery Timeline: Specific delivery dates are agreed upon in individual Project Orders.
4.2 Delay Clause: If the Service Provider delays delivery by more than 15 business days due to their fault, the Client may claim a penalty of 0.5% of the project value per day of delay, up to 10% maximum.
4.3 Acceptance Testing: The Client has 10 business days to review and accept deliverables.
4.4 Change Requests: Any changes to agreed scope require written change orders and may affect timeline and cost.

Section 5 — Intellectual Property Rights
5.1 Upon full payment, all custom code and deliverables become the exclusive property of the Client.
5.2 The Service Provider retains ownership of pre-existing tools, frameworks, and libraries used in development.
5.3 The Service Provider may use generic technical approaches and methodologies in future projects.
5.4 Both parties acknowledge open-source components are subject to their respective licenses.

Section 6 — Warranty
6.1 The Service Provider warrants that all deliverables will:
    (a) Function materially as specified for 90 days after delivery
    (b) Be free from material defects in workmanship
    (c) Not infringe third-party intellectual property rights
6.2 Warranty Remedy: For any warranty breach, the Service Provider shall correct defects at no additional cost.
6.3 Warranty Exclusions: Warranty does not cover issues caused by Client modifications, misuse, or third-party software.

Section 7 — Liability
7.1 The Service Provider's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim.
7.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.
7.3 This limitation does not apply to breaches of confidentiality obligations.

Section 8 — Term and Termination
8.1 This Agreement commences on the signing date and continues for 1 year, automatically renewing annually.
8.2 Either party may terminate for convenience with 60 (sixty) days written notice.
8.3 Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach if the breach is not cured within 14 days of written notice.
8.4 Termination for Cause by Client: If the Service Provider fails to deliver agreed services for 30+ consecutive days without valid reason, the Client may terminate immediately.
8.5 Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.

Section 9 — Data Protection
9.1 The Service Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Client data.
9.2 Client data shall not be used for any purpose other than service delivery.
9.3 Upon termination, all Client data shall be returned or destroyed within 30 days.
9.4 Data breaches must be notified to the Client within 72 hours of discovery.

Section 10 — Force Majeure
10.1 Neither party is liable for failures caused by circumstances beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, or infrastructure failures.
10.2 The affected party must notify the other within 5 days of a force majeure event.
10.3 If force majeure continues for more than 90 days, either party may terminate the agreement.

Section 11 — Governing Law and Disputes
11.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
11.2 Disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiations within 30 days.
11.3 Unresolved disputes shall be submitted to the Tashkent Commercial Court.

Effective Date: March 1, 2025
