EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

Article 1 — Parties
This Employment Contract ("Contract") is entered into between:
- EMPLOYER: TechCorp LLC, a company incorporated under the laws of Uzbekistan, Registration No. 123456
- EMPLOYEE: John Smith, Passport No. AA1234567

Article 2 — Position and Duties
2.1 The Employee is hired for the position of Software Engineer in the Engineering Department.
2.2 The Employee shall perform duties including: developing software applications, code review, participating in technical design discussions, and documentation.
2.3 The Employee reports directly to the Head of Engineering.

Article 3 — Compensation
3.1 The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of USD 2,000 (two thousand US dollars), payable on the 25th of each month.
3.2 Performance bonuses may be awarded quarterly based on Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) set by management.
3.3 All payments are subject to applicable taxes and deductions required by law.

Article 4 — Working Hours
4.1 Standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with a one-hour lunch break.
4.2 Overtime work exceeding 40 hours per week shall be compensated at 1.5 times the regular hourly rate.
4.3 Remote work may be permitted up to 2 days per week subject to manager approval.

Article 5 — Annual Leave and Benefits
5.1 The Employee is entitled to 21 (twenty-one) calendar days of paid annual leave per year.
5.2 Medical insurance coverage is provided for the Employee and immediate family members.
5.3 The Employee is entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year with full pay.
5.4 Public holidays as defined by Uzbekistan labor law are observed.

Article 6 — Probation Period
6.1 The Employee shall serve a probationary period of 3 (three) months from the start date.
6.2 During the probation period, either party may terminate this contract with 3 days written notice.
6.3 Upon successful completion of probation, the Employee receives full employment status.

Article 7 — Confidentiality
7.1 The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information, trade secrets, and business data of the Employer.
7.2 This confidentiality obligation remains in force for 2 (two) years following termination of employment.
7.3 Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and legal action.

Article 8 — Termination
8.1 Either party may terminate this Contract with 30 (thirty) days written notice.
8.2 The Employer may terminate the Contract immediately for cause, including but not limited to: gross misconduct, fraud, theft, or repeated violation of company policies.
8.3 Upon termination, the Employee shall return all company property within 5 business days.
8.4 Severance pay equal to one month's salary shall be paid for each year of service, up to a maximum of 6 months.

Article 9 — Intellectual Property
9.1 All work products, inventions, and developments created by the Employee in the course of employment are the exclusive property of the Employer.
9.2 The Employee waives all moral rights to works created during employment.

Article 10 — Non-Compete
10.1 For a period of 12 (twelve) months following termination, the Employee shall not work for direct competitors of the Employer.
10.2 Direct competitors are defined as companies providing software development services in Uzbekistan with revenues exceeding $1 million annually.

Article 11 — Dispute Resolution
11.1 Any disputes arising from this Contract shall first be subject to mediation for 30 days.
11.2 If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved through arbitration under Uzbekistan commercial arbitration rules.
11.3 The governing law of this Contract is the law of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Signed on: January 1, 2025
